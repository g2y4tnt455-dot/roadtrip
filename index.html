Pacific Coast Road Trip

July 9–15, 2025 · Portland OR to Sacramento CA

7Days
~800Miles
♿Accessible

♿ Accessibility

  • All stops have paved paths, drive-up viewpoints, or elevator access
  • Restrooms at every major stop
  • Call hotels ahead to confirm ADA room availability
  • All ocean views accessible from boardwalks or overlooks

🕐 Crowd Timing

  • Ecola & Haystack Rock: arrive before 10 AM
  • Cannon Beach gets busy after 11:30 AM
  • Redwoods: afternoons are quieter
  • Bandon viewpoints: lighter than downtown

🌅 Best Sunsets

  • Day 2: Seaside Promenade boardwalk
  • Day 4: Bandon Beach — sea stacks glow at golden hour
  • Day 6: Mendocino Headlands — best of the trip
