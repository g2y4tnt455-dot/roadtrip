♿ Accessibility Notes

  • All stops rated Excellent accessibility — paved paths, drive-up viewpoints, or elevator access throughout
  • Restrooms available at every major stop
  • Always call hotels ahead to confirm ADA room availability
  • Beach sand areas are optional — all ocean views accessible from boardwalks or overlooks

🕐 Crowd Timing Tips

  • Ecola State Park & Haystack Rock: arrive before 10 AM for best photos
  • Cannon Beach downtown gets busy after 11:30 AM in summer
  • Newport Bayfront: moderate crowds late afternoon — still fine
  • Bandon Beach viewpoints: stay near paved areas, lighter than downtown
  • Redwoods: afternoons are quieter than mornings

🌅 Best Sunset Spots

  • Day 2: Seaside Promenade — watch from the boardwalk
  • Day 4: Bandon Beach — sea stacks glow at golden hour
  • Day 6: Mendocino Headlands — best sunset of the trip
  • Day 7: Last look at Mendocino before heading inland

🚗 Driving Notes

  • All routes follow Hwy 101 south — one of the most scenic drives in the US
  • Day 7 turns inland via CA-128 through Anderson Valley — beautiful drive
  • Gas up in larger towns: Astoria, Newport, Florence, Crescent City
  • Cell service can be spotty — download offline maps before leaving
